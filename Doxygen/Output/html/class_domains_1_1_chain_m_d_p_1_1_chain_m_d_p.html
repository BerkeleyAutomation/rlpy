Detailed Description

Author:
Developed by Alborz Geramiard Nov 20th 2012 at MIT #

A simple Chain MDP
s0 <-> s1 <-> ... <-> sn
Actions are left [0] and right [1]
The task is to reach sn from s0. Optimal policy is always to go right
