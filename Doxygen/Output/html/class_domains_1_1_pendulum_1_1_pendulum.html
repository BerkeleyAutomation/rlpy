Detailed Description

Author:
Robert H. Klein, Alborz Geramifard at MIT, Nov. 30 2012

This is the parent class for pendulum-type problems with two (continuous) states only, and three discrete possible actions.

Note:
Despite the naming convention in common use here and in the community, the dynamics of the Pendulum actually correspond to the 4-state system with a pendulum on a cart, with x and xDot states omitted. Thus, actions still take the form of forces and not torques.

State: [theta, thetaDot].
Actions: [-50, 0, 50]

theta = angular position of pendulum (relative to straight up at 0 rad), and positive clockwise.
thetaDot = Angular rate of pendulum

Actions take the form of force applied to cart;
[-50, 0, 50] N force are the default available actions. Positive force acts to the right on the cart.

Uniformly distributed noise is added with magnitude 10 N.

All dynamics and conventions per Lagoudakis & Parr, 2003

Note:
This domain cannot be instantiated; it is a superclass for the specific domains Pendulum_SwingUp and Pendulum_InvertedBalance.
