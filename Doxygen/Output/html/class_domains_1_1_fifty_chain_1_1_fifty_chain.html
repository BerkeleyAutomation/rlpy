Detailed Description

Alborz Geramifard, Robert H.

Klein at MIT, Dec. 21, 2012

50-state chain described by Lagoudakis & Parr, 2003 s0 <-> s1 <-> ... <-> s50 actions are left [0] and right [1] Reward of +1 at states 10 and 41 (indices 0 and 9) Actions succeed with probability .9, otherwise execute opposite action.
